JOB DESCRIPTION MANAGEMENT

At Specky, we want to improve the quality of communication between recruiters and candidates.
By making the job description more intelligent, we want to help improve your candidate response rates and help you increase candidate engagement.

INTELLIGENT
JOB DESCRIPTIONS

Improve Candidates response rates by sharing insights about career prospects, location, team culture and hiring organization.

Traditional Job Specs give a great overview of the role and skills required for the job, but they don't answer career, organization or culture related questions that candidates have at the onset. Specky helps you use your industry know-how to curate and annotate job specs in an extremely easy and effective way to get more high quality candidates interested in your opening.

JOB DESCRIPTION
DISTRIBUTION & MANAGEMENT

Automate the process of sharing job specs; tracking and recording key analytics at each point in the process.

The conventional method of sharing stacks of MS Word job specs with prospects over email in exchange of resume documents is laborious, cumbersome and inefficient. Specky automates and optimizes this channel to make it extremely effective. It communicates on your behalf using specially designed mobile-friendly emails that are geared to provide key insights and have a candidate click through to the spec.

HOW IT WORKS?

  • STEP 1 - UPLOAD OR EMAIL YOUR SPEC
    You can email or upload your existing specs to Specky.
  • STEP 2 - CURATE YOUR SPEC
    Use your expertise and knowledge to make the job specs more meaningful
  • STEP 3 - PUBLISH & MONITOR
    Real time updates help you monitor if a candidate has read your spec and if they are interested.
